# GitHub Education Benefits: Step-by-Step Guide

This guide explains how to apply for GitHub Education benefits as a student or teacher.

## Steps

1. Open GitHub: https://github.com
2. Create a new free account.
3. Enter your email, password, username, and country/region.
4. Go to **Settings** and open **Billing and licensing**.
5. Open **GitHub Education Benefits** to access free developer resources.
6. Start a new application.
7. Select your role in education: **Student** or **Teacher**.
8. Search and select your school/university.
9. In "What is the name of your school?", enter your university name.
10. Enter your university email address.
11. Go to the option to enable **2FA** (two-factor authentication) for account security.
12. Select the type of proof you want to provide.
13. Choose the first option: **Dated school ID**.
14. Enter your university address for ID location details.
15. Submit your application.
16. Check your email and complete all verification steps.1. 

## Important Notes

- Use real and accurate school information.
- Make sure your document is clear and valid before uploading.
- Keep 2FA enabled to protect your account.
- Approval may take some time depending on verification volume.
